# 📌 Overview

**TrustHire AI** is an intelligent recruitment platform designed to connect job seekers with verified recruiters while protecting users from fake jobs, internship scams, phishing recruiters, and fraudulent hiring activities.

The platform combines **Artificial Intelligence, NLP, Resume Analysis, Fraud Detection, and Smart Matching Algorithms** to create a secure and trusted recruitment ecosystem.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Many students and job seekers face:

❌ Fake job postings  
❌ Internship scams  
❌ Fraud recruiters  
❌ Spam and phishing links  
❌ Unrealistic salary offers  
❌ Requests for payment during hiring  

Traditional job portals often fail to verify recruiters properly and cannot detect suspicious job postings effectively.

---
