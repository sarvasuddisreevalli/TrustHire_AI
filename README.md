🚀 Project Title
TrustHire AI
AI-Powered Trusted Recruitment Platform
📌 Overview

TrustHire AI is an intelligent recruitment platform designed to connect job seekers with verified recruiters while protecting users from fake jobs and internship scams.

The platform uses Artificial Intelligence to:

Verify recruiters
Detect fake job postings
Analyze job descriptions
Match candidates with suitable jobs based on skills
Provide secure and trusted recruitment services
🎯 Problem Statement

Many job seekers face:

Fake job postings
Internship scams
Fraud recruiters
Misleading salary offers
Spam and phishing links

Traditional job portals do not properly verify recruiters or detect suspicious job postings.

TrustHire AI solves this issue by using AI-based verification and fake job detection mechanisms.

💡 Solution

TrustHire AI introduces:

Verified recruiter system
AI-powered fake job detection
Resume skill extraction
Smart job recommendation engine
Trust score analysis for job postings
AI chatbot for instant verification
